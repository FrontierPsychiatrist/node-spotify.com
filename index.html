node-spotify

What is it?

node-spotify allows you to control Spotify via your browser! This is extremely useful if you want to play music on one device and control it via another device. A great example is the Raspberry Pi. Imagine attaching it to your stereo and then controling the music via your smarthpone! This is what expensive devices from other manufactures can do (e.g. a Sonos), but it runs on a cheap Raspberry Pi.

Great, what do i need?

To use node-spotify you need

  • A Spotify premium membership (sorry, this is a constraint for using libspotify)
  • A computer running Linux or OSX with internet access (again sorry, no Windows support at this time)
    Preferrably, this is a Raspberry Pi that is attached to your stereo.
  • A modern webbrowser (Chrome, Firefox)
